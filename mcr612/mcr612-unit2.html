Eukaryotic cells communicate with each other through various mechanisms. This communication is essential for coordinating multicellular functions, development, and immune responses.

Cytokines are small proteins that act as signaling molecules in cell-to-cell communication. They play crucial roles in immune responses, inflammation, and cell growth.
