Characteristics of Good Industrial Strains:

7. Product Recovery (Downstream Processing)

After fermentation, the desired product must be separated and purified from the fermentation broth. This process is called downstream processing or product recovery.

Stages of Downstream Processing:

1. Cell Separation (рдХреЛрд╢рд┐рдХрд╛ рдкреГрдердХреНрдХрд░рдг)

Separate cells from the liquid medium (broth).

  • Centrifugation: Spin at high speed to separate cells
  • Filtration: Filter through membranes
  • Sedimentation: Let cells settle naturally
рд╣рд┐рдВрджреА: рдЕрдкрдХреЗрдВрджреНрд░рдг, рдирд┐рд╕реНрдкрдВрджрди, рдЕрд╡рд╕рд╛рджрди
2. Cell Disruption (рдХреЛрд╢рд┐рдХрд╛ рд╡рд┐рдШрдЯрди)

If product is inside cells, break open cells to release it.

  • Mechanical: Homogenization, sonication, bead milling
  • Chemical: Detergents, solvents
  • Enzymatic: Use enzymes to break cell walls
рд╣рд┐рдВрджреА: рдпрд╛рдВрддреНрд░рд┐рдХ, рд░рд╛рд╕рд╛рдпрдирд┐рдХ, рдПрдВрдЬрд╛рдЗрдореЗрдЯрд┐рдХ
3. Product Isolation (рдЙрддреНрдкрд╛рдж рдЕрд▓рдЧрд╛рд╡)

Separate the product from other components.

  • Extraction: Use solvents to extract product
  • Adsorption: Bind product to solid material
4. Purification (рд╢реБрджреНрдзрд┐рдХрд░рдг)

Remove impurities to get pure product.

  • Chromatography: Separate based on size, charge, or affinity

  • Dialysis: Remove small molecules
рд╣рд┐рдВрджреА: рдХреНрд░реЛрдореИрдЯреЛрдЧреНрд░рд╛рдлреА, рдХреНрд░рд┐рд╕реНрдЯрд▓реАрдХрд░рдг, рдбрд╛рдпрд▓рд┐рд╕рд┐рд╕

Increase product concentration and remove water.

  • Evaporation: Heat to remove water
  • Ultrafiltration: Filter to concentrate
  • Freeze-drying: Remove water under vacuum
  • Spray drying: Convert to powder
